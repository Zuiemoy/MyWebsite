Peanut Butter Sandwitch

Ingredients

  • Sourdough bread - 2pc
  • Brie cheese - as long as it can fit on breads
  • Banana - 1pc
  • Bacon - 2pc
  • Raw Sugar - 1g
  • Special peanut butter (Extra sauce can be stored in fridge for using next time)
    • Soy sauce - 20g
    • Honey - 25g
    • Peanut butter - 125g
    • Apple vinegar - 15g
    • chilli powder - 1g
    • Onion powder - 2g
    • garlic powder - 2g

Steps

Caramelised Banana
  1. Cut banana into half
  2. Fry it on low heat with raw sugar until caramelised
  3. Turn the other side, and continue frying it on low heat until golden

Special Peanut Butter
  1. Well-mix soy sauce, honey, and warm water
  2. Slowly add in peanut butter, stir until well mixed
  3. Add in apple vinegar, chilli powder, garlic power, and onion powder
  4. Stir until all well mixed
Bacon
Fry it until crispy

Brie Cheese
Cut into small chunks

Assembly
  1. Toast sourdough breads
  2. Spread Special Peanut Butter on toast
  3. Stack Caramelised Banana, and Bacon on toast
  4. Add on Brie Cheese chunks
  5. Enjoy :)
